# Privacy Policy

**Kinlog** | Last updated: March 2026

## Overview
Kinlog is a fitness dApp built on Solana. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect
- **Motion data**: Camera input is processed locally on your device for squat detection. No video is recorded or transmitted.
- **Wallet address**: Your Solana wallet address is used to mint NFT badges and record on-chain activity.
- **Fitness stats**: Workout counts, streaks, and goals are stored in Firebase Firestore to provide app functionality.

## How We Use Your Data
- To count squat repetitions using AI-powered pose detection
- To mint NFT badges as proof of your fitness achievements on Solana
- To track your daily goals, streaks, and points

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
- Workout stats are stored in Firebase (Google Cloud).
- NFT minting transactions are recorded on the Solana blockchain, which is public by nature.

## Camera Usage
Kinlog uses your device camera solely for real-time pose detection. No images or video are stored or transmitted.
