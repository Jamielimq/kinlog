Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

1. Introduction

Kinlog ("we", "our", or "the app") is a fitness tracking application built on the Solana blockchain. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Kinlog on Android (Solana Seeker).

By using Kinlog, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Camera Access
Kinlog uses your device camera solely for real-time pose detection to count squat repetitions. No video is recorded, stored, or transmitted. All pose analysis occurs locally on your device using MediaPipe.

Workout Data
We store the following data in Firebase Firestore:

  • Total squat counts and workout history
  • Daily and weekly fitness goals and progress
  • Streak counts and points balance
  • NFT badge achievement records

Wallet Address
When you connect a Solana wallet to mint NFT badges, your public wallet address is used solely for on-chain transactions. We never access your private keys or seed phrase.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your personal fitness progress and history
  • Track daily goals, streaks, and achievements
  • Mint NFT badges to your Solana wallet upon achieving milestones
  • Improve app performance and functionality

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your workout data is stored in Firebase Firestore (Google) and is used solely to provide app functionality. NFT minting transactions are recorded on the Solana public blockchain by nature of the technology.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your workout data is retained as long as you use the app. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. On-chain NFT records on the Solana blockchain cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase security rules and HTTPS encryption. Camera data is never stored or transmitted — all processing occurs on-device in real time.

7. Children's Privacy

Kinlog is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of Kinlog after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
